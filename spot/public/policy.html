Privacy Policy for Playlist Spot

Last Updated: 2/6/2024

Welcome to Playlist Spot, the macOS app designed to enhance your Spotify experience by providing quick access to your playlists, insights about the currently playing track, audio visualizations, and more, all with the ease of one-click functionalities.

Respecting Your Privacy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. Playlist Spot is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we do not collect, our practices regarding any information obtained, and how we use the Spotify API to enhance your app experience.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal Information: Playlist Spot does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require users to provide any personal data to use the app.
  • User Data Collection: The app does not collect or store any user data. This includes, but is not limited to, browsing history, playlist content, or any other personal usage data.

Spotify API Access

  • User Consent: Playlist Spot accesses the Spotify API using a key generated after obtaining user consent. This key is used solely for the purpose of providing the app's features to the user and is not used for any form of user tracking or data collection.
  • Audio Visualization: We use the key to display audio visualization within a WebKitUI on an external website. This process does not involve the collection or transmission of personal user data.

No Data Sharing

  • Third-Party Sharing: Playlist Spot does not share any information with third parties. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to share or sell to any entity.

Security

  • We are dedicated to protecting the security of our users. While we do not collect personal information, any interaction with the Spotify API and external websites for audio visualization is conducted in a secure manner to ensure user privacy and data protection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
